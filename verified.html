VERIFIED BY TWILIO

Web Platform

Twilio has positioned itself as a leader in the Cloud Communications space by providing a suite of APIs and solutions for messaging, voice, video, chat, notifications among other channels.

Having the infrastructure and the experience building programmatic products, there was probably no better candidate to solve this problem: Robocalls.

The problem: Robocalls have got out of control over the last few years, putting in risk people's identity and safety. While deliverability rates in our platform have increased, for some particular use cases, recipients are not engaging via SMS or taking phone calls. Why? people don't trust unknown numbers anymore. While they can be legit, there's no way for us to know.

Goals: At a high-level we wanted to:
- Increase response and engagement rates from our communication channels.
- Provide a seamless verification and end to end branded communication experience.
- Make it flexible so it can be used by any existing and future channel.

The solution: With clear goals and everyone from product to design and development on board. I created a new section in the Console navigation, called "Business Profile" where customers were going to provide all the information necessary to get a "verified" status.

On the other side, I designed a separate flow for brand creation and channel connections, this way, customers would go through a global verification, then they would start creating brands and connect to a particular channel independently, giving more flexibility and avoiding users go through verification multiple times in the future.

The result: We tested with some existing customers behind a flag and found the overall verification process working well, except for the need to add more explicit copy and clearer alert messages.

On the other hand, channel integrations seemed to be a lit bit confusing, mostly due to the current legacy flow.

An iterated version was launched during SIGNAL conf where it had a great reception and high interest from customers and attendees.
