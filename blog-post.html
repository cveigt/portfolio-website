What I Learned by Releasing a Freebie

A few months ago, I’ve launched one of my side projects. Same as the first one, I decided to share it with the community for free. It was a long time since I worked on a mobile project different from the apps of the company I work for, so I thought, why not do a mobile screens set. Having in mind that I only work on these side projects some nights of the week and on weekends, It could not be something too big that takes so much time. First, I quickly started to define a generic set of views; Sign up, Profile, Settings… you know. A week after, I have finished 6 views and I realized I can get some others done.
